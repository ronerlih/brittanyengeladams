Brittany Engel-Adams

works at the intersection of dance and technology


'

Brittany Engel-Adams is an artist working at the intersection of dance and technology. Her work is usually live, and uses online data and software, to peel off layers of our culture, as a way to discover our humanity. She sets live or recorded performances and installations - that mimic, play, and transform our way of viewing the world - using code. Brittany holds an MFA from The University of the Arts' School of Dance Program at The (ICI) Institute Chorégraphique International, CCN Montpellier, Occitanie / Pyrénées Méditerranée (FR). Her work was commissioned by Galerie Thaddaeus Ropac (FR), Symphony Space (NYC), Guggenheim Museum’s Works & Process (NYC), Movement Research Performance Journal (Issue 55), New Media Caucus Symposium at Virginia Tech (VA). She served on the faculty at New York University and Rutgers University. Brittany is a 2023 artist in residence at Harvestworks (Technology, Engineering, Art and Music Lab).
