Brittany Engel-Adams

New Media Artist


'

Brittany Engel-Adams is a new media artist working at the intersection of dance and technology. Typically, her work is live, and uses online data, and software to consider urgent questions that might challenge our humanity in the 21st century. Ranging from climate change, to the rise of new disruptive technologies, such as artificial intelligence. In the coming decades, the twin revolutions of bio-tech and info-tech, merge into a singular scientific tsunami, this could undermine our conception of humanity, and shake the humanist foundation of modernist civilization. Brittany has a Master's degree from The University of the Arts' School of Dance Program – at The (ICI) Institute Chorégraphique International – CCN Montpellier – Occitanie / Pyrénées Méditerranée (FR). Her work has been presented at Galerie Thaddaeus Ropac – Paris Pantin (FR), Symphony Space – New York (NY), The Guggenheim Museum’s Works & Process – New York (NY), Movement Research Performance Journal / (Issue 55), The New Media Caucus at Virginia Tech – Blacksburg (VA) – among other venues. Brittany is a 2023 Harvestworks Artist in Residence Awardee.
