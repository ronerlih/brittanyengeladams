Brittany Engel-Adams


Brittany Engel-Adams is an interdisciplinary artist, whose experimental approach to dance and sound is typically live. She works with motion-capture technology, artificial intelligence, and robotics to explore how bodies are shaped by technology, and the role of bodies in our collective future. Forthcoming: ‘BodyMouth/Ielele’ – Future Bodies Symposium at Virginia Tech. Past: Publication of ‘Architecture of Memory’ – Movement Research Performance Journal No. 55.
