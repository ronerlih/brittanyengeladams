Brittany Engel-Adams

Artist


'

Brittany Engel-Adams is a new media artist who works at the intersection of dance and technology. She received her Master's degree from The Institute Chorégraphique International, CCN Montpellier Occitanie in Pyrénées Méditerranée, France. Brittany’s work considers the ways in which the body is shaped by the social and geopolitical contemporary moment to question the role of bodies in our collective future. Her work was presented by The New Media Caucus at Virginia Tech (Blacksburg Virginia), Movement Research Performance Journal (Issue 55), Works & Process – Guggenheim Museum (New York), Symphony Space (New York), Galerie Thaddaeus Ropac (Paris Pantin France), among other venues. Brittany is a 2023 Artist in Residence at Harvestworks (Technology, Engineering, Art and Music Lab).
